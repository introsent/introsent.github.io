About the Project

Forefront is a 32-player VR battle game from Triangle Factory, the studio behind Breachers and Hyper Dash. Players take sides in a war between O.R.E. and the local armies and governments, fighting across large, semi-destructible environments built for squad-scale combat — available on Meta Quest, Steam, PlayStation, and Pico.

I started at Triangle Factory in February 2026, first as an intern and now in a student position, working on optimization and graphics programming for Forefront. Most of that work doesn't fit neatly into a single feature — it's the kind of thing that shows up as a build that's smaller, a frame that's faster, or a crash that no longer happens. The posts below go into the specifics.
