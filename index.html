Ivans Minajevs

As a C++ programmer with a specialism in graphics and physics programming, I am currently studying game development at the Howest University of Applied Sciences in Belgium. My professional interests lie in the fields of mathematics and physics.

Mainly, I am interested in the integration of my expertise in graphics programming, including lighting physics, volumetric effects, thermal and fluid visuals. Additionally, I am keen to explore opportunities in game / engine programming, particularly in designing and implementing rigid body dynamics, character physics, collisions, vehicle and object simulation.

In addition to my full-time role, I am a part-time private tutor in mathematics. I work with students from my home country, preparing them for national examinations and assisting them with scientific projects.

I am eager to work in team and always learn and discover something new from experienced people in the industry.
